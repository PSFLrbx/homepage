PSFL Games Rulebook

Server Settings

Public Listing: OFF
Fumble Chances: 1
Mercy Rule: 40 pts
Play Clock: 25 seconds
Quarter Length: 4 minutes
Replays: ON
Time of Day: As Scheduled
Weather: As Scheduled

Game Mechanics

Diving: ON
Hitstick: ON
Sprinting: ON
Truck: OFF
Pass Break Up: OFF
Mercy Rule: OFF

Players & Game Rules

Anyone can fumble.
Only one Center/OL per play.
Players cannot play while unsigned unless given permission by @lu or @pka.
No exploits that provide an unfair advantage.
Suspended players cannot play regular season games.
Macro usage is allowed.
Alt accounts require permission from a (+)Executive (not on the player’s team).
After kickoff, set play clock to :pclock 50 for setup (including halftime/overtime kickoff).
Only 1 fake per half is allowed.
A webhook must be added; otherwise, the game won’t count (exceptions apply).
If a team owner/coach hosts without a webhook (and no eligible refs), the team gets an FFL.
If the webhook is unofficial, the game requires a redo (owner/coach: FFL; ref: fired).
If a referee shuts down the server during a game, they are suspended, fired, and the game must be redone.
If a team admin shuts down the server, their team receives an FFL.
Mercy Rule cannot be denied.
Games must be 7v7 - 8v8 (exceptions only in forced cases, e.g. 7v6, 8v7). The referee will use :pclock 100 to allow proper player count; otherwise, it’s an FFL.
Only 2 QB runs per drive.

Challenges

If the auto-ref call is disputed, coaches (+AC) may challenge. The referee reviews the challenge and issues a verdict. Teams have three challenges per game; a successful challenge returns the challenge without penalty. Challenges may only be issued for:

Infraction of Rules
False Incompletions
High boost/in-bounds calls
Incorrect calls on body contact
Ineligible Players (not signed, suspended, alt-account)

Penalties

Grounding — QB throws blatantly out of range. [-15]. Grounding in own endzone or near endzone results in a safety.
Delay of Game — Automatically called; may be reverted if an NR was called but the ref failed to react. [-5]
Lag Sack — Penalty for a defensive end’s lag sack (QB lag does not count); results in down replay.
Double Blocking — Two blockers blocking 5 yards behind the line; negated on run plays or if QB is sacked. [-5]
Triple Blocking — Three or more blockers forward toward their own TD; only negated if QB is sacked or ball is run (except fakes). [-10]
Milking — Unreasonable delay unless clock is 00:30 or lower in 2nd/4th quarter. [-10]
Headriding — Stacking on the head for more than 2 seconds. [-5]
More than one fake per halftime — Results in a 2-point deduction and skipping the special play.
