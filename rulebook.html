Challenges

If the auto-ref system fails or an official deems a call incorrect, a challenge may be requested by a Head Coach or Assistant. Challenges apply to:

  • Determining receiver inbounds status
  • Addressing questionable lag sacks
  • Verifying excessive blocking

Penalties

Delay of Game

5yd penalty and loss of down when the play clock expires without a snap.

Too Many Men

5yd penalty if a team exceeds the player limit on the field.

Ineligible Receiver

Results in an incomplete pass and a replayed down if a receiver steps out of bounds.

Lag Sack

5yd penalty for the defense and an automatic first down for the offense when a lag sack occurs.

Intentional Grounding

15yd penalty and loss of down for an invalid passing attempt.

OL Rollout

5yd penalty and loss of down if an offensive lineman crosses the line on a pass play.

Illegal Trucking & Hit Stick

10yd penalty and replayed down if an illegal weight is used. (Truck: 185–255 lbs; Hit Stick: 165+ lbs)

Holding

5yd penalty and replayed down if blocking exceeds 5 yards from the line of scrimmage before the snap.

Block in the Back

10yd penalty and replayed down for initiating a block from behind.

Defensive blitz limits:

  • 8v8: Up to 4 players
  • 7v7: Up to 3 players
  • 6v6: Up to 2 players

AFK Rule

A team incurs a false start penalty if a player is idle for over 3 minutes.
