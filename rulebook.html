Challenges

If the auto-ref system fails or a call is disputed, a challenge may be requested by a Head Coach or Assistant. Challenges apply to:

  • Receiver inbounds status
  • Questionable lag sacks
  • Excessive blocking

Penalties

Delay of Game

5yd penalty and loss of down when the play clock expires without a snap.

Too Many Men

5yd penalty if the team exceeds the player limit on the field.

Ineligible Receiver

Results in an incomplete pass and a replayed down if a receiver steps out of bounds.

Lag Sack

5yd penalty for the defense and an automatic first down for the offense when a lag sack occurs.

Intentional Grounding

15yd penalty and loss of down for an invalid pass attempt.

OL Rollout

5yd penalty and loss of down if an offensive lineman crosses the scrimmage line on a pass play.

Trucking & Hit Stick

10yd penalty and replayed down.

Holding

5yd penalty and replayed down if blocking exceeds 5 yards before the snap.

Block in the Back

10yd penalty and replayed down for initiating a block from behind.

Defensive blitz limits:

  • 8v8: Up to 4 players
  • 7v7: Up to 3 players
  • 6v6: Up to 2 players

AFK Rule

A false start penalty is incurred if a player is idle for over 3 minutes.
