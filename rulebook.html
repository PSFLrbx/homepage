PSFL Games Rulebook

Server Settings

  • Public Listing: OFF
  • Fumble Chances: 1
  • Mercy Rule: 40 pts
  • Play Clock: 25 seconds
  • Quarter Length: 4 minutes
  • Replays: ON
  • Time of Day: As Scheduled
  • Weather: As Scheduled

Game Mechanics

  • Diving: ON
  • Hitstick: ON
  • Sprinting: ON
  • Truck: OFF
  • Pass Break Up: OFF
  • Mercy Rule: OFF

Players & Game Rules

  • Anyone can fumble.
  • Only one Center/OL is allowed per play.
  • Players cannot participate in regular season games while unsigned unless given permission by @lu or @pka.
  • Exploits that provide an unfair advantage are prohibited.
  • Suspended players are not allowed to play regular season games.
  • Macro usage is allowed.
  • Alt accounts require permission from a (+)Executive (not on the player’s team).
  • Play clock must be set to :pclock 50 after kickoff and for subsequent drives (including halftime and overtime kickoff) to allow proper setup.
  • Only 1 fake per half is allowed.
  • A webhook must be added; otherwise, the game will not count. Exceptions exist.
  • If a team owner/coach hosts without a webhook (and no eligible referees are assigned), their team results in an FFL.
  • If the webhook is not the official PSFL webhook, the game requires a redo (team owner/coach servers result in FFL; referee servers lead to firing).
  • If a referee shuts down the server during a game, they will be suspended, fired, and the game must be redone.
  • If a team admin shuts down the server, their team receives an FFL.
  • Mercy Rule cannot be denied.
  • Games must be 7v7 - 8v8 (exceptions only in forced cases, e.g. 7v6, 8v7). The referee will use :pclock 100 to allow the team with fewer players to fill the field; otherwise, it results in an FFL.
  • There are only 2 QB runs per drive.

Challenges

If the auto-ref call is disputed, coaches (+AC) can challenge. The referee will review the challenge and issue a verdict. Teams have three challenges per game. Successful challenges do not reduce the challenge count. Challenges may only be issued for:

  • Infraction of Rules
  • False Incompletions
  • High boost and in-bounds calls
  • Incorrect calls on body contact
  • Ineligible Players (not signed, suspended, or alt-account)

Penalties

  • Grounding — QB throws blatantly out of eligible receiver range. [-15]. Grounding in own endzone or near endzone results in a safety.
  • Delay of Game — Automatically called; may be reverted if an NR was called but the ref failed to react in time. [-5]
  • Lag Sack — Penalty for a defensive end's lag sack; QB lag doesn’t count. (Down replayed.)
  • Double Blocking — Two blockers blocking 5 yards behind the line of scrimmage; negated on a run play or if QB is sacked. [-5]
  • Triple Blocking — Three or more blockers forward toward their own TD; only negated if the QB is sacked or the ball is run (except fakes). [-10]
  • Milking — Unreasonable delay unless clock is 00:30 or lower in 2nd or 4th quarter. [-10]
  • Headriding — Stacking on the head for more than 2 seconds. [-5]
  • More than one fake per halftime — Results in a 2-point deduction (if scored) and skipping the special play.
